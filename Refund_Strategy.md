## Positioning Statement

AMZBoosted is the **only privacy-first browser extension** for Amazon Seller Central automation. Unlike Helium 10 and Jungle Scout — which upload your sensitive business data to their servers — AMZBoosted runs entirely in your browser. Your data never leaves your machine.

**What we are:** A Chrome extension that plugs directly into Seller Central, reads your active session, and automates the manual export and scheduling work that wastes hours every week.

**What we are not:** A cloud SaaS. A data aggregator. A tool that requires API keys or MAPI linking.

## Privacy & Security Messaging

**Primary privacy statement:**
> "100% of your metrics stay on your machine. We can't see your product data even if we wanted to."

**Four trust points:**
1. **Local-Only Storage** — All scraped data, reports, and settings stored 100% locally on your machine.
2. **Military-Grade Encryption** — AES-256 encryption + SHA-256 data signatures.
3. **Secure Backup & Restore** — Switch computers without losing data. Encrypted backup restorable with your unique account key.
4. **Zero API Keys** — No MAPI linking. No SP-API credentials. Reads your active browser session directly.

**Trust badges:**
- 100% Secure · Browser Extension · No API Keys · 14-Day Free Trial · No Credit Card Required
