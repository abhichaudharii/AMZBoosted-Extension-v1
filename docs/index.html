Questions? We've Got Answers

When will AMZBoosted launch?

We're targeting Q1 2026 for public launch. Waitlist members get early access 2 weeks before. We'll keep you updated every step of the way via email with product previews and beta testing opportunities.

How much does it cost?

Starting at $49/month for solo sellers with unlimited accounts and reports. Agency plans start at $99/month. The first 500 waitlist members get a lifetime 20% discount, making it just $39/month forever. No credit card required to join the waitlist.

Is my Amazon account data safe?

Absolutely. We use OAuth-only authentication (Amazon SP-API standard) and never store your Amazon credentials. Your data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). We're SOC 2 Type II compliant. Privacy is our #1 priority.

Can I use this with multiple accounts?

Yes! AMZBoosted is built specifically for multi-account sellers and agencies. Connect unlimited Amazon accounts across all marketplaces (US, UK, CA, DE, FR, IT, ES, JP, AU, and more) from one unified dashboard. Switch between accounts instantly.

What reports can be automated?

Every Seller Central report type can be automated:

  • • Orders & Payments Reports
  • • Inventory & FBA Reports
  • • Returns & Reimbursements
  • • Brand Analytics & Search Terms
  • • Advertising Reports (PPC)
  • • Business Reports & Traffic

Schedule them daily, weekly, or monthly. Reports are automatically downloaded and organized in your dashboard.

What integrations do you support?

We integrate with popular tools like Google Sheets, Slack, Discord, Email (SMTP), Webhooks, and Zapier. Export your data to any format (CSV, Excel, JSON) or connect via our API. More integrations are added based on user requests.

What kind of support do you provide?

Early access members get priority support via email, live chat, and dedicated Slack channel. We also provide comprehensive documentation, video tutorials, and weekly onboarding calls. Our average response time is under 2 hours during business hours.
